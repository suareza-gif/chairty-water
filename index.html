Real Impact: Meet Rani

Every morning, she used to set out on a long walk under the hot sun, carrying heavy containers to collect water for her family. The journey took hours, leaving little time for school and little energy for play. The water she carried home was often unsafe, but it was all they had.

Today, her story is different. Thanks to your generosity, her village has a clean water well right in the heart of the community. Instead of walking miles, she now walks just a few steps. Instead of worrying about sickness, her family is healthier and stronger. And instead of missing out on an education, she sits in a classroom, learning, laughing, and dreaming about her future.

Clean water has given her family more than health — it has given them hope. It has restored time, opportunity, and joy. And it’s all because of supporters like you who believed that every child deserves the chance to grow up with safe water and big dreams.
